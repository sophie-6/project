Dear Jack,

Can you believe it's already been a whole year!? And even though you fell for me first, we both know that I fell for you harder.
And I'm still falling - into your arms as you hold me tightly, or into your gaze that holds me captive. Every sensation from you - every touch, every kiss, every hug, every cuddle - brings an irreplacable feeling of safety and security. Even the ghost of your kisses on my forehead bring me comfort late at night. You're a constant reminder that I'm not alone; that I will never have to be alone again.
You remind be that I am so grateful for being able to love - and be loved - by you.

You are so gorgeous both inside and out. You're like the night sky - always there, always beautiful. The stars twinkle like your eyes when I look into them, the moon glows like your smile when I see you, and the navy blue is deep and rich like your heart. Sometimes at night I like to go outside, and I used to think how endlessly pretty the stars were, but now I see you in them. Slight breezes brush past me like your fingers through my hair and the moonlight washes over like your soothing voice.

I used to wish on shooting stars, and now I know they really do come true.
I used to dream of love, but I don't need to anymore.
My dreams have come true, and they are you.

No matter what fights we have, no matter what challenges we face, I will always love you.
I will always be here for you as you have for me.

I think I finally understand the meaning of how loving someone until it hurts will never hurt as much as never getting to love them at all.

I love you so much, Jack.
I love that I am yours, and you are mine.
I love you - forever and always.
